What is Design Thinking?

Design thinking is a methodology for innovation that integrates creative and analytical approaches, and requires collaboration across disciplines. A deep empathy for the user is the foundation of design thinking, and the process often begins out in the field where we develop an understanding of the way customers see their world. Design thinking leverages generative tools to imagine what their future could be like and analytical tools to determine the best way to achieve that future. Moving forward, the process is biased towards action. Design thinking encourages prototyping fast and often, and measuring and iterating until an outcome that integrates market insight, business strategy and technological capability is reached.
